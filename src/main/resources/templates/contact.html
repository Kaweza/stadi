Artist Song Submission

To distribute your music on ShuttamusicZ, follow these steps:

  1. Ensure your music meets our submission guidelines, including audio/video quality and content standards.
  2. Prepare song information: title, artist name, genre, release date, and cover art.
  3. Email your submission to submissions@shuttamusicz.com with the subject line: "Song Submission – [Artist Name]". Include the song as an attachment or a downloadable link.

Song Takedown Request

If you’re a rights holder and need to request a takedown of a song from our platform, please:

  1. Email copyright@shuttamusicz.com with subject: "Takedown Request – [Song Title]". Include:
    • Your contact information
    • Proof of ownership or copyright
    • A clear explanation for the request

We review all takedown requests promptly in accordance with our policies and applicable laws.
